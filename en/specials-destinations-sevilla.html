Specials



Sevilla, San José Mansion, SPAIN



Bringing the Past to Life

A Spanish course immersed in Seville's Golden Century


All our Spanish immersion programs are individualized and offered at a premium setting.

But if you want to take things to the next level, our LUXURY EXPERIENCE will just do that.

Offered by the dedicated ANDERS LANGUAGES®' staff and owners, we do not involve 3rd parties, thus guaranteeing your privacy and an unforgettable experience.


The Spanish classes


Depending on the number of class hours per day you want and the chosen setting (individual, two-on-one or small group), one, two or three personal teachers will be assigned to you.

The classes can be tailored to your, your and your partner’s, your family’s or corporate staff needs.

The Experiences


Your Spanish instructors will also be your local guides when visiting Seville’s landmarks, museums or when exploring the local gastronomy.

The best way to practice your Spanish in an informal way but with the help of your instructors.

Check out all the experiences
The Concierge services


During class time, activities and excursions your Spanish instructors will take care of you.

However, if you need any help during your free time, our concierge service will be at your disposal: making reservations, advice on things to do, finding a chef for a dinner party or cooking class, groceries…

What makes the luxury experience different from our regular Spanish courses?



FULL FLEXIBILITY

- Start any day of the week.

- Decide about your day schedule.

- Choose the number of immersion days.



LUXURY ACCOMMODATIONS IN PRIVATE APARTMENTS

- One, two and three-bedroom apartments located in a 17th century Casa Palacio mansion.

- Located in the vibrant historical city center of Seville.

- Concierge service.

All the other features of an ANDERS LANGUAGES®’ immersion remain the same: individualized classes, meals and intercultural experiences with your instructors...
