Our team

TEXT OK, excepted instructors


All ANDERS LANGUAGES® instructors have two things in common: they are experts in their field and will keep you motivated throughout the course!

Some instructors have an educational background and will take care of the technical and formal aspects of your Spanish language training: grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation...

Other instructors have a business or cultural background and will take care of the activities and informal aspects of the Spanish language.

Their experience in different aspects of life will allow you to discuss social, business, legal or political topics in Spanish, while practicing your listening and speaking skills in an "out of the classroom" environment.
