Intercultural Experiences

The intercultural experiences offer you the possibility to use your Spanish during activities and excursions with your instructors.

You can add one, two or all the experiences. You can also choose one or two intercultural experiences and spread it/them over 2, 3 or more Spanish immersion weeks.

The Intercultural Experiences are offered during class time and are considered as such: your instructors will be with you and you will continue using your Spanish with professional support.





6/6

Ground transportation & welcome lunch

Our drivers can pick you up at any location in Mexico City, including the airport (MEX). The driver will also handle you over the keys to enter the residence and room.

If you include the welcome lunch (or dinner), an instructor will receive you at the immersion residence, show you arround and take you out for a meal.

And of course, after your stay, we can drive you back.

Please note that if you don't any of the services above, check-in will be self-serve, after 4:30 pm.
