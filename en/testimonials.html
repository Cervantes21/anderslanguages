Our students have one thing in common: they are all sophisticated adults!

Although we occasionally receive young people in need of a Spanish immersion program, college and high school students are not our target audience.

The average age of our guests is 48, and 52% are female and 48% are male.

One third of our students are business professionals, while the rest are learning Spanish for personal reasons.

Languages bring people closer together. That is why we are very proud of the 39 nationalities that have visited us since 1998.
