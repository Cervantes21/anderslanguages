Terms and conditions of sale


ONLINE

The sales and cancellation terms below DO NOT apply for the SPANISH IMMERSIONS and SPECIALS

Back to bookings

GENERAL


1. Online classes are offered from Monday to Friday, between 8 am and 4 pm CST (UTC/GMT -6 hours, no DST). Subject to availability of instructors when scheduling. Please use a time zone converter to find the time difference with your country

2. Your pack of online classes should be used within 2 months after payment.

3. You can cancel up to 12 hours before your class. No-shows and cancellations 12 hours or less before the class will be charged

4. You can schedule classes up to 15 business days in advance.

5. Once we have received your payment, a Spanish instructor will contact you within the next 48 hours (except weekends and holidays).

6. You can request a full refund within the next 24 hours following your first online class. After that period, refunds are not possible.

7. The credit for on-site Spanish immersions will be valid for an immersion scheduled no later than 12 months after payment of your first online class

8. The credit cannot exceed 50% of the total cost of your on-site Spanish immersion.

9. The discount for the onsite Spanish immersion is NOT valid for the SPECIALS.

10. Payments for online programs cannot be transferred to on-site programs. Missed online classes cannot be offered on-site.

11. You will be working with a team of two or three ANDERS LANGUAGES instructors from Mexico and/or Spain. It is not feasible to select a specific instructor.

12. There are no classes offered from December 25 to January 1.



USE OF YOUR DATA:


1. We only request information we need to organize your language class.

2. The information we receive is stored on our secured server and its secured back-up on the cloud.

3. Your data will NEVER be shared with, sold to, or rented to 3rd parties.

4. We will always honor any request to update or remove any information. Such requests should be sent by via email.
