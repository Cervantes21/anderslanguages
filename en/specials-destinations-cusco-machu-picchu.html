Non-Peruvian citizens and residents are exempt from 18% sales tax. However, the Cusco Special is put together by Anders Languages Mexico. Tip:

Flights to Cusco usually require a layover in Lima. You might want to stay one or two nights in Peru's capital to recover from your flight and experience the city's extraordinary gastronomy.

If you want to arrive in Cusco one or two nights prior to the start of the SPECIAL, you can stay at the CASONA. Please request this when booking, so we can take advantage of the special fees we received for accommodation
