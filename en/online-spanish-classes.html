ONLINE SPANISH CLASSES

Tailor-made and communication-based!

Learn Spanish from the convenience of your home or office with the same instructors who teach at our schools in Spain and Latin America.


The online classes in a nutshell



The online classes in a nutshell

You will work with the best: dedicated, experienced, and professional Anders Languages instructors. Some of them have been members of our team since our first days, way back in 1998.

If you decide to take your Spanish learning to the next level with an on-site Spanish course at our immersion residences, this will also be a great way to meet your Spanish instructors before you visit us.



We make classes to fit your needs

If you want to prepare for an exam (DELE, SIELE, etc.), or if you want to learn Spanish for business or medical use, your instructors will be happy to adapt the curriculum to your needs and objectives.



You learn at your own time and pace

You decide the number of hours and when you want to have classes. A high teacher-availability is guaranteed.



Experience the culture!

Our instructors are from Mexico, Spain, and several other Latin American countries. Through them, you will be able to experience the culture behind the Spanish language...



Save up to 50%...

50% of what you invest in your online Spanish classes will be converted into a credit that you can use when booking an on-site Spanish immersion.

What to expect?



A tailor-made, conversation-driven, one-on-one class via ZOOM.

Schedule and start your class with two simple clicks.

Access to our course materials, the same materials used at the immersion residences in Spain and Latin America.

We know most apps on the market and will advise on what's best for you if you want to add self-study to your Spanish language learning.

You will be working with a team of two or three ANDERS LANGUAGES instructors, allowing you to experience different teaching styles while maintaining a consistent, structured curriculum. We guarantee high teacher availability, making scheduling classes smooth. Rest assured that your instructors are colleagues and will report your advance to each other.

You can schedule your class anytime between 8 am and 4 pm CST (UTC/GMT -6 hours, no DST), from Monday to Friday. Use a time zone converter to find the time difference with your country.
