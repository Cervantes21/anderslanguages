5/6

Intercultural Experiences

The Intercultural Experiences offer you the opportunity to practice your Spanish during activities and excursions with your teachers.

They are offered during class time and are considered as such: your instructors will be with you and you will continue using your Spanish with professional support.

Each Intercultural Experience includes a module of 2 half days or 1 full day of excursions and/or activities.

You can include one, two, three or four modules. However, we recommend a maximum of one module of intercultural experiences per week.




6/6

Ground Transportation & Welcome Lunch

Our drivers can pick you up at any location in Mexico City, including the airport (MEX). The driver will also give you the keys to enter the residence and your room.

If you include the Welcome Lunch (or Dinner), an instructor will meet you at the Immersion Residence, show you around, and take you out to eat.

And of course, we can drive you back at the end of your stay.

Please note that if you don't book any of the above services, check-in will be self-service between 4:30 pm and 8:30 pm.
