Accommodation

TEXT OK


The amenities and privacy of a hotel...

The warmth and informal feel of a homestay.

An important part of your Spanish language immersion program is the accommodations at the ANDERS LANGUAGES®' residences.

They will guarantee that you will be exposed to the Spanish language in a friendly and professional way, without the informality of a host family or the impersonality of a hotel or apartment.

A hotel or apartment offers the advantage of high privacy and comfort levels. But regardless of how comfortable your hotel is, it is impersonal. You might feel isolated from what's happening around you.

On the other hand, living with a local host family is a great way to use your Spanish and learn more about the local culture. However, since you are a guest in someone else's home, you need to adapt to the house rules and be prepared to compromise the privacy and comfort levels you are used to.

The residences at ANDERS LANGUAGES® offer the best of both worlds: the comforts and services of a hotel, along with the warmth and familiarity of a homestay.

Amenities & services


  • All rooms with air-conditioning / heating
  • Drinks and snacks 24/7
  • Single rooms with private bathrooms
  • Room service
  • Classrooms inside the immersion residence
  • Common areas for our guests: terraces, living room, pool, garden, kitchen...

Rooms and meals


At our residence, we serve homemade meals , that reflect the regional gastronomy of the area. These meals are served in our patio with our other guests and instructors.

It's a great opportunity to chat in Spanish about your day and experiences.

Our meals are made with fresh ingredients and are as balanced as possible.

If you have any special diet restrictions, please let us know.

Coffee and refreshments are available free of charge at any time.

All guest rooms are private and include a bathroom, smart TV, air conditioning, heating, room service, and WiFi.

For demanding guests, we offer upgraded rooms and suites that feature king-sized beds, espresso machines, mini-bars, and large work desks.

Concierge services




Meet & Greet

Our driver will meet you at the airport, train station, hotel, or Airbnb. The meet-and-greet service makes it easy for you to get to the immersion residence. We will also be happy to drive you back.



Welcome lunch or dinner

Upon arrival at the immersion residence, a Spanish instructor will welcome you, show you around and invite you for lunch or dinner, depending on your arrival time.



Guest plan

If you want to invite someone who won't be studying Spanish, we will make sure they have a great time sightseeing, relaxing at the pool, or playing golf while you study Spanish.



Weekends

The weekend is the perfect time to relax. You could go for a swim, play some golf, or visit nearby cities and museums. We would be happy to help you make plans for your weekend.
