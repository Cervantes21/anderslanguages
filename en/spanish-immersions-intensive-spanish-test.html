Our approach


Intensive and personalized...

The classes


All day long you will be immersed in the Spanish language through a combination of formal classes and activities to practice what you have learned.


ForONE program

A one-on-one approach is the best way to achieve short-term results.

The curriculum will be based on your goals, interests and learning profile.

ForTWO program

For two people who want to study together: you and your partner, a friend, a colleague...

Both participants need a similar linguistic level, but if that is not the case, you can still opt to have individual instructors within the same program.

ForALL program

For companies, organizations, families or a group of friends seeking a tailor-made program for 3 or more people visiting together.

The activities


Learning Spanish wouldn't be complete without using the language in an outside-the-classroom environment.

All our programs already include having all your meals and breaks with your instructors, which is an informal way to further practice your Spanish in an outside-the-classroom environment but in a structured way.

Moreover, most programs also include at no cost the possibility to use your Spanish in an extensive way during a number of activities with your Spanish instructors.

A city tour, having coffee at a local bar... there are plenty of things to do offering you the possibility to practice what you learned during class time.

While the Spanish classes are individualized, the activities take place with your own instructor and other guests: if there are 4 students, there will be 4 instructors.

Your day


You can choose from a 6, 9 or 12-hour-per-day schedule. The recomened schedule will depend on your goals and the duration of your immersion.

All schedules include breakfast and lunch with your instructors and other guests, each with his/her instructor. The 12-hour schedule also includes dinner with your instructors.

Your day is a combination of formal, individualized classes and informal, conversation driven activities. That allows us to keep you motivated, all day long:

Your morning (6, 9 and 12 hour schedules)
9:00 am - 9:40 am: breakfast with instructors
9:40 am - 2:00 pm: classes
2:00 pm - 3:00 pm: lunch with instructors

The afternoon (9 and 12 hour schedules)
4:30 pm - 7:45 pm: classes or activities

And your evening (12 hour schedules)
7:45 pm - 8:45 pm: evening activities
8:45 pm - 10:00 pm: conversation driven activities

Immersions start any week of the year.
